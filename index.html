  • Date: From September 2020 to May 2021
  • Client: ZTP , A consulting firm
  • Category: Innovation

T-Wall Project

A project led during my 2nd year at ITEEM

The client Crouzet Agencement asked us to create a packaging for his product the "T-Wall", a writable and moving, "T" shaped board, aimed to restaurants, for the children. The client wanted an ecological and reusable packaging, so we decided to reuse it as a soundproofing wall. For the aspect design we used corks, which is a grate insulation and played on their shape and colors to make drawings as you can see above. The client was very happy of this solution, he kept it and he is now developing the idea.

  • Date: From September 2019 to May 2020
  • Client: Crouzet Agencement
  • Category: Product Design
